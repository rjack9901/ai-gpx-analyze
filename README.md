## 🌟 Key Features
- **Precision Analytics:** Instantly extracts Pace, Heart Rate, and Cadence from any GPX source (COROS, Garmin, Strava).
- **Lap Breakdown:** Automatically segments workouts into 1km laps with detailed metric tables.
- **Data Visualization:** Interactive trend charts powered by Chart.js to track performance stability and cardiac drift.
- **Gemini AI Integration:** One-click "AI Coach" prompt generator, optimized for deep technical analysis on Gemini Web.
- **Privacy-First:** 100% client-side processing; your sports data never leaves your browser.

---

## 🛠 How it Works
1. **Upload:** Open the app and upload your GPX file.
2. **Review:** Check the auto-generated summary dashboard and lap tables.
3. **AI Analysis:** Click **"Copy AI Coach Prompt"** to copy the professionally crafted analysis command.
4. **Coaching:** Click **"Go to Gemini Web"**, paste the command, and get your professional training report!
